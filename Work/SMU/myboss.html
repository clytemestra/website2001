Bonnie Wheeler is an extraordinary woman -- charismatic, driven, insane -- who is the director of the Medieval Studies program at Southern Methodist University in Dallas. She has recently started up Scriptorium Press, a small non-profit academic press whose mission is to seek out strange new scholarship that big publishers wouldn't touch with a ten-foot pole. Not trash, mind you -- she is also the editor of Arthuriana, the journal of the International Arthurian Society, series editor of the New Middle Ages books published by St. Martin's Press, director of the International Joan of Arc Society, and a highly respected scholar.
