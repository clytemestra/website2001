Drivel, Dravel

Oh, and there was the not-so-casual remark of someone close to me, "You know, Fred, I've known you for ten years and consider you one of best friends, but I don't really know anything about you." So, here goes.

I am extremely opinionated -- in the bad way -- outrageous opinions and absolutely no rational arguments to back them up. This is one reason all of my friends live 1K+ away.

I am engaged to Jason Flowers, and if I were to take his name upon marriage, I would be Jennifer Flowers. I find this highly amusing.

I hate Elmo. But I think Pikachu is the greatest. David Bowie is God and always will be. I once ran off a roommate by playing Space Oddity over and over and over and over. I have three cats, Baby Kitty, Dante, and Vergil (who goes by "Peep"). All of my friends live at least 1000 miles away from me, no matter where I live. I hate the mountains, and the people who live in the mountains hate me. I am more nocturnal than my cats. Growing up in a less than cosmopolitan area, I learned how to discover literature and philosophy through popular culture (watch an episode of Star Trek and try to track down all the literary references, I dare you). I believe that science fiction is a mask worn by writers enabling unhindered exploration of religion, philosophy, and some of the more interesting questions and aspects of life in a widely accessible public forum. I believe that most of what is on television is utter trash. I watch TV anyway.
