Just the Facts

My name is Jennifer Guillory, but I and many of my friends and family know me and think of me as Fred. I am a sometimes editor, occasional writer, on-demand researcher, and frequent web designer working and living in the Dallas metro area ("D/FW" in local parlance, with me living somewhere in the "/"). My education is in Latin, with a little bit of Greek, and classical literature.

History/Story of My Life

One of the things that I have learned on my classical journey is that the word 'history' in Greek (historia) has as its original meaning 'story,' and only later came to be used in the sense with which we are familiar. So, in the tradition of Herodotus, 'the father of history,' I will hereafter copiously intersperse commentary with the dry, boring facts of my existence.
